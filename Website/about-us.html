Educating international students on Australian bicycle safety
And helping them learn about the numerous bikepaths around them
Our Motivation
Cycling is an excellent mode of transport for University students, as it promoties physical and mental wellbeing, and is a convenient and cost effective way of getting around. However, cycling can be dangerous, with 744 bicycle fatalities on Australian roads from 2001-2020 (Bicycle Network Australia, 2021).
Our Mission
To improve the bike riding experience of every day Australians by making the locations of various bikeways more accessible and promoting safe cycling practices.
